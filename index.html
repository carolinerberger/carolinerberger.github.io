Semester Project · A22

MindAligned

MindAligned is an AI-powered co-founder matching platform that replaces surface-level profile matching with deep behavioral compatibility analysis — capturing how founders actually think, communicate, and handle conflict under pressure.

Built as part of USC's entrepreneurship curriculum, the project addresses one of startups' most overlooked failure points: the human dynamic between co-founders.

AI / Voice Analysis Behavioral Psychology Startup Ecosystem USC Entrepreneurship

The Problem

Co-founder conflict is the #1 people problem in startups.

"The human element is the single greatest point of failure."
65% of startups fail due to co-founder conflict — not funding, not market fit.

Existing matching tools optimize for credentials: GitHub commits, LinkedIn titles, résumé keywords. None of them answer the question that actually matters — can these two people build something together under real pressure?

Our team observed this gap directly through USC's founder community, where 30 applicants with strong credentials applied for 10 spots at an invite-only founder dinner — and no existing tool could predict who would actually collaborate well.

The Solution

Matching on resonance, not résumés.

MindAligned replaces the form with an AI voice interview. The platform captures decision-making under pressure, responses to disagreement, and commitment signals — then analyzes both what you say and how you say it.

🧠 Behavioral DNA

Extracts personality archetypes through voice tonality and linguistic patterns during pressure-test interviews.

📊 Conflict Predictive Modeling

Uses data to simulate how two potential partners will handle equity disputes and scaling challenges before they arise.

🎯 Ranked Compatibility Output

Surfaces co-founder matches mapped to risk tolerance, work style, commitment level, and conflict response.
