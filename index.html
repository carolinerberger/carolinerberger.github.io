MindAligned

Find the right co-founder,
not just the most impressive resume.
Connect beyond credentials. Build what matters.

MindAligned is an AI-powered co-founder matching platform that connects people through mind, ideas, and values — not credentials. It replaces surface-level profile matching with a 20-minute AI voice interview that captures how founders actually think, communicate, and behave under pressure.

AI / Voice Analysis Behavioral Psychology Co-Founder Matching USC Entrepreneurship B2B SaaS
The Problem

Startups don't fail from bad ideas. They fail from bad partnerships.

65% of startups fail due to co-founder conflict
3.3× more efficient — teams with high trust
5.1× more likely to deliver results with aligned teams
"65% of startups fail due to co-founder conflict." — Professor Noam Wasserman, Harvard Business School

How are teams formed today? Networking and gut feel. Resume and pedigree matching. These methods completely ignore the four traits scientifically proven to determine startup survival: risk tolerance, work style, commitment level, and conflict response.

Research from PNAS and Scientific Reports across 10,000+ startups confirms it — the signals that actually predict whether two people can build together are the exact ones no current platform is measuring.

The Solution

We measure the variables that break co-founder teams.

Input

20-minute AI voice interview capturing decision-making, conflict response, and commitment signals

→
Mechanism

Dual-layer analysis: Content (what they say) + Voice (tone, hesitation, consistency)

→
Output

Ranked co-founder matches with top alignment dimensions and flagged conflict risks

🧠 Behavioral DNA

Personality archetypes extracted through voice tonality and linguistic patterns during pressure-test interviews.

⚡ Pressure-Test Scenarios

AI-led interview scenarios designed to reveal how founders respond to conflict, ambiguity, and high-stakes decisions.

📊 Conflict Predictive Modeling

Simulates how two potential partners will handle equity disputes and scaling friction — before they arise.

🎯 Ranked Compatibility Output

Matches mapped across risk tolerance, work style, commitment level, and conflict response — not just overlapping skills.

Why now, why us

Voice + LLM analysis now makes it possible to quantify how people think under pressure. Universities and accelerators run structured founder cohorts but still rely on unstructured team formation. Every existing solution matches on resumes and profiles — not behavioral compatibility. MindAligned is infrastructure for programs that already exist, not a consumer marketplace. Every cohort generates proprietary outcome data that improves matching over time — a dataset no competitor has.

My Contribution

I built the research foundation that made MindAligned credible.

A platform claiming to predict human compatibility can't just sound good — it has to be grounded in real science. That was my job. I went deep into behavioral psychology literature and AI linguistic analysis research to understand which signals actually predict interpersonal compatibility, and translated that into frameworks the team could design and build around.

What I owned:

Behavioral Psychology Research

Investigated how personality archetypes, attachment styles, conflict patterns, and communication tendencies predict long-term co-founder compatibility — going beyond surface-level personality tests to find what the science actually says.

AI Linguistic Analysis

Researched how voice tone, hesitation patterns, and linguistic consistency can surface deeper compatibility signals — the things people reveal about themselves without meaning to. This directly informed the dual-layer analysis at the core of MindAligned's mechanism.

Research-to-Product Translation

Synthesized academic findings into actionable inputs for the platform's interview design and matching logic — bridging the gap between what the science shows and what the product needed to do.
